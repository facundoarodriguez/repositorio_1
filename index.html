The Band and Members

Owl Cult Club was born in the 2020 pandemic in the city of Hurlingham by the guitarist Facundo Rodriguez as absolute composer of all the pieces, while the production was left in the hands of Mauro Dordoni. At first it started as a project to pass the time, but soon it began to have more relevance and commitment as a result of the reproductions of the demos uploaded to SoundCloud. Today we are working on our first professional EPs.

The Homestudio

The studio was founded in the city of San Miguel, with only a PC specialized in audio processing programs and a second-generation Focusrite Scarlett audio card. With the advancement in equipment, the studio was moved to Ciudadela where we have our own space for recording, although at the same time we continue working from our studios at home. Nowadays anyone can set up a home studio.
